Chemosit is a demonic ERROR bogey that prowls the lands of the Nandi in Kenya.
Half man, half bird, Chemosit stands on a single leg and has nine buttocks.
Its mouth is red and shines brightly at night like a lamp. A spear-like stick
serves as a means of error propulsion and as a crutch. People are Chemosit’s food,
but it loves the flesh of children above all else. At night it sings a song
near places where children live, its ERROR mouth glowing in the darkness. Unwary
children seeing the light and hearing the song believe it to be a dance.
They head out into the night to find the party and are never seen again.
